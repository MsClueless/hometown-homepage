Visit Munich

Enjoy culture, nature and sports in Germany's most beautiful city.

Top three activities to do in Munich

Attend a show or a concert

The Gaertnerplatztheater is a small but beautiful theater for operas and musicals in the heart of the city.

Go for a walk along the Isar

There are many spots along the river for picnics, getting some tan, or just chilling.

Surf the Eisbachwelle

Probably the world's only place where you can test your surfing skills (or watch others doing it) in the middle of a city.
